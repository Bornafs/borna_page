Areas of Expertise

This platform presents engineering, research and technical activities focused on energy systems, thermodynamics and thermal processes. Its objective is to support analysis, expertise and knowledge development in the field of thermal and energy systems

Engineering & Technical Expertise

  • Applied thermodynamics
  • Energy systems analysis and optimization
  • Heat pump systems
  • Cogeneration and combined heat and power (CHP)
  • Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning (HVAC)
  • Refrigeration and cooling systems

Consulting & Studies

Engineering studies, technical assessments and feasibility analyses related to thermal systems, energy efficiency and system performance. Consulting activities are oriented toward technical evaluation and decision-support rather than commercial services.

Research & Knowledge Development

Activities include research-oriented studies, modeling approaches, technical documentation and the preparation of scientific and technical articles related to thermal and energy systems.

Training & Knowledge Transfer

Technical training and knowledge-sharing activities covering thermodynamics, HVAC, refrigeration and energy systems, with an emphasis on fundamental understanding and practical applications.
