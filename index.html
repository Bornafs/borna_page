Engineering & Energy Platform

This platform is dedicated to engineering, research and technical development in the fields of energy systems, thermodynamics and thermal processes.

The objective is to analyze, improve and optimize energy systems through modern engineering methods, performance analysis and applied research.

  • Energy efficiency and optimization
  • HVAC & thermal systems
  • Heat pumps and refrigeration
  • Renewable and hybrid energy systems
  • Research, modeling and technical studies

The platform also integrates training, technical documentation and engineering support activities in collaboration with industrial partners.
